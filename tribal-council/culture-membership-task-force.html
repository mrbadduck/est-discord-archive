Eric Mirowitz 10/22/2024 9:57‚ÄØAM
Hey guys, I was blown away by my own experiences and some reflections shared by others over the course of the sabbath dance weekend, and have been meditating on the question of what our "unique thing" is that roots our community. We've talked about how other communities like ours typically start with either a building or a rabbi as the centerpiece, but for us, it was the community/culture of east nashville. I think the radical inclusivity and sense that everyone along all the spectrums of jewishness is welcome and can find community here without judgement is what makes us special. I came up with this idea "East means ALL" as a play on "all means all" but leaning into the specifically inclusive values of east nashville. I was also thinking about the teaching that Lawrence shared about how the vav prefix in hebrew is traditionally translated/understood as "and" but a modern feminist interpretation takes it to be inclusive of the full spectrum in between the two things getting anded together. I combined these ideas into this concept and I'm curious how this resonates with you all.
