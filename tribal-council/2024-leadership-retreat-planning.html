I haven't sent out the leadership retreat email yet because I keep thinking about what the purpose of it should be. I keep coming back to the idea of sustainability. Since our last retreat, we have gotten new leaders into the fold but others have had to take steps back for various reasons - so it still feels like three people propping up the organization in some ways right now. Sustainability means thinking about: how do we function as a leadership team? How do we have two people leading each program? How do we make Shabbats feel easier (like the host one pager)? How do we plan ahead more? How do we structure our org? Instead of the board making these changes on the side and then rolling them out, I would argue that making these changes the purpose of the retreat - identifying what needs to be done and then setting priorities and then assigning the top priorities - is what will make more people feel empowered to step up.
